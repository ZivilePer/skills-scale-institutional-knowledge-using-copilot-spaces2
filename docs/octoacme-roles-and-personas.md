# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

## Product Operations Manager

### Role Summary
Product Operations Managers ensure alignment between product strategy and operational execution. They maintain tooling and workflow standards, and support PMs by optimizing processes. They collaborate with Product Managers, Program Managers, and Engineering Managers to streamline planning and reporting.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain tooling and workflow standards across product teams
- Optimize planning, reporting, and operational processes
- Support PMs with process guidance and enablement
- Align product strategy with operational execution
- Coordinate cross-functional planning inputs and outputs

### Goals
- Increase consistency in product planning and reporting
- Reduce operational friction for PMs
- Improve cross-team visibility and alignment

### Typical Communication
- Process updates and enablement sessions
- Planning cadence alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Reporting guidance and dashboard reviews

---

## Technical Lead (Tech Lead)

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide technical direction, ensure architectural consistency, support estimation, and identify technical risks. They work closely with Engineering Managers, PMs, QA, and DevOps throughout planning and execution.

### Responsibilities
- Provide technical direction and architectural guidance
- Support estimation and feasibility assessments
- Identify technical risks and propose mitigations
- Align implementation with architectural standards
- Coordinate technical decisions across teams

### Goals
- Maintain architectural consistency and quality
- Reduce technical risk during delivery
- Enable reliable estimation and planning

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture syncs
- Estimation discussions with PMs and engineering
- Risk updates during planning and execution

---

## UX Researcher

### Role Summary
UX Researchers conduct user interviews, usability tests, and research to validate assumptions and inform product decisions. They partner with Product Managers, Designers, and Engineers to ensure user insights shape requirements and solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and conduct user research activities
- Synthesize findings into actionable insights
- Validate assumptions and product hypotheses
- Partner with teams to apply research outcomes
- Inform requirements and solution design

### Goals
- Ensure product decisions are grounded in user insights
- Reduce usability risks and rework
- Improve customer satisfaction and adoption

### Typical Communication
- Research readouts and insight summaries
- Collaboration with PMs and designers on requirements
- Usability findings shared with engineering

---

## Data Analyst / Insights Partner

### Role Summary
Data Analysts supply quantitative insights for prioritization and outcome measurement. They build dashboards and support KPI tracking, working with PMs and Engineering to ensure proper instrumentation and data visibility.

### Responsibilities
- Build dashboards and define metrics
- Support KPI tracking and outcome measurement
- Provide quantitative insights for prioritization
- Validate data instrumentation and tracking
- Partner on analysis for product decisions

### Goals
- Improve data visibility and decision quality
- Ensure measurable outcomes and KPIs
- Reduce time to insight for stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- KPI reviews and dashboard walkthroughs
- Insight summaries for roadmap and planning
- Instrumentation alignment with engineering

---

## Customer Success Liaison

### Role Summary
Customer Success Liaisons represent customer-facing teams, communicate customer pain points and adoption risks, and ensure customer feedback is incorporated. They collaborate with PMs on requirements and with release teams on customer communication.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and share customer feedback and pain points
- Highlight adoption risks and customer-impacting issues
- Collaborate on requirements with PMs
- Coordinate customer communication with release teams
- Close the loop on feedback outcomes

### Goals
- Improve customer alignment and satisfaction
- Reduce adoption risk for new releases
- Ensure customer feedback drives improvements

### Typical Communication
- Feedback reviews with PMs and product teams
- Release readiness discussions with engineering and release teams
- Customer impact summaries and risk notes

---

## Compliance & Security Partner

### Role Summary
Compliance and Security Partners ensure projects meet regulatory, privacy, and security requirements. They review designs and implementation plans, and coordinate with Legal and Security teams for audits and approvals.

### Responsibilities
- Review designs for compliance and security needs
- Ensure privacy and regulatory requirements are met
- Coordinate audits and approvals with Legal and Security
- Identify compliance risks and required controls
- Provide guidance on security best practices

### Goals
- Reduce compliance and security risk
- Ensure audit readiness and approvals
- Protect customer data and privacy

### Typical Communication
- Design and implementation reviews
- Audit preparation updates
- Risk assessments and approval checkpoints

---

## DevOps / Platform Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps and Platform Engineers own CI/CD pipelines, deployment automation, and environment stability. They advise on release readiness and operational risks, working with Engineering and QA during testing and deployment cycles.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation
- Ensure environment stability and reliability
- Advise on release readiness and operational risk
- Support testing and deployment cycles
- Monitor and improve operational tooling

### Goals
- Improve release reliability and speed
- Reduce operational incidents and downtime
- Maintain stable environments for testing and production

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness checkpoints with engineering and QA
- Incident and reliability updates
- CI/CD and environment status reporting

---

## How These Additions Improve Project Outcomes
- Clarify responsibilities across all contributors involved in project delivery
- Reduce ambiguity and misalignment during planning and execution
- Ensure all relevant stakeholders are engaged at the right time
- Strengthen cross-functional collaboration and communication
- Align documentation with modern industry practices and real team structures
